Dependency Chain Design

  • Vertical line connects parent → child quests in a chain
  • Dashed horizontal arm extends further right per nesting depth
  • Opacity decreases with distance from the running quest — blocked/queued items fade
  • Dot color matches status: green (running), amber (blocked), grey (queued), checkmark (done)
  • Independent chains have no connecting line between them
  • Running quests glow subtly to draw the eye
