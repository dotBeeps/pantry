# dragon-parchment

**Status:** 🔥 beta
**Code:** `berrygems/extensions/dragon-parchment.ts`

## What It Does

Central panel authority for hoard extensions. Manages creation, positioning, focus cycling, and cleanup of floating overlay panels via a `globalThis` API. Other extensions register panels through dragon-parchment rather than managing them independently.
